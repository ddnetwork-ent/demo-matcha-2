About Matcha Haven

Bringing authentic Japanese matcha culture to modern world

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Matcha Haven emerged from a deep appreciation for Japanese tea culture and a desire to share its transformative benefits with world. Our journey began in the serene tea fields of Uji, Kyoto, where we forged relationships with family-owned farms that have been perfecting matcha cultivation for generations.

Each cup we serve represents centuries of tradition, combined with modern innovation. Our master baristas undergo extensive training in traditional Japanese tea ceremony techniques, ensuring that every beverage maintains the integrity and spirit of authentic matcha preparation.
